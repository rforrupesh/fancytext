FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is this tool?

It’s a free online font generator that lets you type text and instantly turn it into fancy Unicode styles.

Where can I use these fonts?

You can paste them into Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, Discord, and messaging apps like WhatsApp and Telegram.

Will screen readers read these fonts?

Not always. Fancy Unicode text may not be accessible, so use plain text for clarity when needed.

Why does the text sometimes show boxes or question marks?

Some devices or apps don’t support every Unicode character. If that happens, try another font style from the list.
