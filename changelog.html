Changelog & Latest Updates

Stay informed about the newest features, improvements, and bug fixes on Font Generator. We're constantly working to enhance your experience and bring you more creative text tools!

Version 1.3 - July 7, 2025

  • New Tool: Bold Text Generator! Added a tool to convert text into Sans-serif Bold, Serif Bold, and Bold Italic styles.

Version 1.2 - July 4, 2025

  • New Tool: Glitch Text Generator! A new tool for creating distorted and corrupted text effects.

  • New Tools: Cool Text Generator & Fancy Text Generator! Expanded our collection with more stylish and decorative text options.

Version 1.0 - June 28, 2025

  • Initial launch of Font Generator website.

Why We Have a Changelog

A changelog is a chronological, human-readable record of all notable changes made to a project. For Font Generator, it serves as a transparent way to communicate our ongoing development and commitment to improving the tools and user experience. It allows you to see what's new, what's been fixed, and how the website is evolving.

We believe in continuous improvement and value your feedback. This page reflects our dedication to regularly updating the platform with new features and enhancements based on user needs and technological advancements in Unicode text manipulation.
