Make Your Own Brat Album Cover

Welcome to the ultimate Brat Generator. Inspired by Charli XCX's iconic "Brat" album aesthetic, this tool helps you create custom artwork featuring the signature lime green background and lo-fi bold typography that defined a summer of club culture.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Enter Text: Type your message in the text area. All text is automatically converted to lowercase to match the "Brat" style.
  • Style Effects: Choose Blur for the classic hazy look, Scribble for a scratchy underground vibe, Mirror for a flipped effect, or Clean for sharp contrast.
  • Download: Click the download button to export a 1080×1080 PNG, perfect for profile pictures or social posts.

Why is the "Brat" Look so Popular?

The "Brat" aesthetic is a masterclass in intentional lo-fi design. By using bold text on a high-visibility lime green background (#8ace00), the design rejects traditional polish in favor of raw, immediate energy. It's accessible, instantly recognizable, and perfectly suited for the meme era.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the hex code for Brat Green?

The specific lime green used is #8ace00. Our generator uses this as the default background color.

What font does Charli XCX use on Brat?

The album art uses Arial Narrow Bold. Our generator replicates this using a bold sans-serif with compressed letter spacing to ensure it looks right on all devices.

Is this tool free?

Yes! This generator is completely free for personal use, allowing fans to join the "Brat Summer" movement with their own custom text.
