Character Counter

A character counter is a simple online tool that counts the total number of characters, words, sentences, paragraphs, and lines in any text you type or paste. It updates in real time — no button to click, no waiting.

What Does This Tool Count?

This tool gives you 8 different stats instantly:

  • Characters — total characters including spaces
  • No Spaces — characters without spaces
  • Words — total word count
  • Sentences — split by . ! ?
  • Paragraphs — separated by blank lines
  • Lines — every line break counted
  • Spaces — total space characters
  • Reading Time — estimated at 200 words per minute

Why Use a Character Counter?

Different platforms have strict text limits. Knowing your character or word count helps you stay within those limits without guessing:

  • Twitter / X — 280 characters per tweet
  • Instagram bio — 150 characters
  • Google meta description — 150–160 characters recommended
  • SMS messages — 160 characters per message
  • LinkedIn posts — 3,000 characters
  • YouTube titles — 100 characters (60 shown in search)

How to Use

Just paste or type your text in the box above. All stats update instantly. Use the Copy button to copy your text, Clear to reset (your text is saved to History automatically), and the History button to restore any previous session.

Letter Density

The Letter Density section shows how often each letter appears in your text and what percentage of the total it makes up. This is useful for writers, cryptographers, and anyone analyzing writing patterns.

Top Words

The Top Words section shows the most repeated single words, 2-word phrases, and 3-word phrases in your text. It helps you spot overused words and improve the quality of your writing.

Undo & Redo

Made a mistake? Use the Undo and Redo buttons in the toolbar, or use the keyboard shortcuts Ctrl + Z to undo and Ctrl + Y to redo — same as any text editor.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it count spaces as characters?

Yes. The Characters stat includes spaces. The No Spaces stat shows the count without them.

Is my text saved anywhere?

No. Your text never leaves your browser. It is stored only in your browser's local storage so it persists if you refresh the page.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute, which is typical for most adults reading online content.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes. The tool is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Is there a character limit?

No. You can paste as much text as you want — there is no limit imposed by this tool.
